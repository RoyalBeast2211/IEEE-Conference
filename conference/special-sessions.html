Special Sessions

SEFET 2026 invites professionals in the area of Conference Topics to propose Special Sessions (SS) to be held on 6th IEEE International Conference on Sustainable Energy and Future Electric Transportation (SeFet 2026) in Nagpur, India during 8th-11th July 2026.

Benefit of SS: Special sessions provide the opportunity to focus in detail on particular emerging topics, which are not reflected in the list of conference tracks or represent specific working fields where researchers would like to meet and discuss advances.

Submit Your Proposal

Please send your proposal in MS Word format to:
Dr. A. Narendrababu
ieeesefet2026@vnit.ac.in  |  anbabu@eee.vnit.ac.in

Proposal Content

Your special session proposal should include the following information:

  • Special session title and acronym
  • Brief description of the special session (200-300 words)
  • Names, affiliations, email addresses, and short CVs of the organizers
  • List of potential contributors and paper titles (if available)
  • Rationale for organizing this special session

Special Session Organization

Special session organizers are responsible for:

  • Promoting the special session to potential authors and contributors
  • Ensuring that submitted papers are relevant to the special session topic
  • Coordinating with the conference technical program committee
  • Chairing the special session during the conference

Paper Submission

Papers submitted to special sessions will follow the same submission process as regular conference papers:

  • All submissions must follow the IEEE conference paper format
  • Papers will undergo the same peer review process
  • Accepted papers will be published in the conference proceedings
  • At least one author of each accepted paper must register for the conference

Review Process

All special session proposals will be reviewed by the conference technical program committee. The committee will evaluate proposals based on relevance to the conference topics, novelty, potential impact, and the qualifications of the organizers. Accepted special sessions will be announced on the conference website.

Requirements

For a special session to be included in the final conference program:

  • A minimum of 8 accepted papers is required
  • All papers must meet the quality standards of the conference
  • At least one organizer must be present to chair the session

Important Dates

  • Special Session Proposal Deadline: 07th Dec 2025
  • Notification of Acceptance: 10th Dec 2025
  • Paper Submission Deadline: 31st Jan 2026 15th Feb 2026
