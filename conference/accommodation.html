Accommodation Details

Guidelines, Tariff, and Payment Procedures for IEEE SeFet 2026 Participants

🏨

Campus Accommodation

We will make every effort to accommodate outstation participants in the VNIT Guest House / Hostel Guest Rooms, subject to availability and payment as per the Institute's rules.

Allotment is strictly on a first-come, first-served basis.
Payment must be made at the time of room allotment/check-in.
Accommodation will be confirmed only upon receipt of payment.

🌐 Payment Method: VNIT Online Portal

Follow the steps below to pay online through the VNIT online payment portal:

A
Visit the VNIT online payment portal at pay.vnit.ac.in/home.
B
On the homepage, click on "Online Payment".
C
Select the payment category "Other Misc. Fines and Payment".
D
Select "Hostel Room Rent for Students" as the payment category.
E
Enter your Paper ID in the Registration Number field.
F
In the Remarks field, enter "SEFET 2026 Accommodation Fee".
G
Complete the payment process and save the payment receipt for future reference.

*Allocated per person per day as per Institute rules.

⚠️

Important Note

If you wish to stay outside the VNIT campus, please make your own accommodation and conveyance arrangements. The organizing committee is only responsible for facilitating bookings within VNIT hostels/guest rooms based on institutional rules and availability.
