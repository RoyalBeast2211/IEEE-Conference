IEEE Industry Applications Society (IAS)

Advancing the Design, Development, and Safe Application of Electrical Systems in Industry and Commerce

About IEEE IAS

The IEEE Industry Applications Society (IAS) is one of the largest special interest societies within the IEEE. It focuses on the unique needs of industry and commerce in the application of electrical systems and electronic devices.

At IEEE SeFet 2026, IAS sponsors a dedicated track showcasing high-impact industrial solutions, green power applications, and advanced motor designs. WIE and IAS events join forces to connect academic research with commercial applications, aiming to accelerate the clean electric transit revolution.

Learn more about IEEE IAS

Event Highlights

  • Technical & Exhibition Sessions

    Showcasing cutting-edge papers on electric vehicle drives, industrial power convertors, charging architecture, and energy storage systems.

  • Industry-Academia Panel

    "Bridging Commercial Needs with Academic Ingenuity" — A high-level discussion on standardizing charging protocols, grid security, and clean transit scaling.

  • Networking & Recruitment

    Meet industry pioneers, discover commercial sponsorships, and explore career directions in advanced power engineering and transportation design.
