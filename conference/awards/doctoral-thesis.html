Outstanding Doctoral Thesis Award

The organizing committee of IEEE SEFET-2026 is soliciting nominations for the IEEE SEFET 2026 Outstanding Doctoral Dissertation Award. The award is intended to recognize the best emerging academic work at the doctoral level that falls under the scope and enhances the mission of sustainable energy and future electric transportation.

Eligibility Criteria

  • The candidate must have completed PhD from a recognized Indian institute/university.
  • The candidate's dissertation must have been successfully defended between 1st January 2024 to 30th April 2026.
  • Candidate must be able to attend and register for IEEE SEFET-2026.
  • The dissertation must be submitted in English.
  • Candidates who received the Outstanding Doctoral Thesis Award at SEFET 2025 are not eligible to apply.

IEEE Policy 4.4.H: Individuals serving on any board or committee involved at any stage of the recipient selection or approval process for an award shall be ineligible to receive, or act as a nominator or reference for that award. This conflict-of-interest limitation shall apply to all awards given by the IEEE or any of its organizational units. The decision of Organizing Committee SEFET-2026 will be final in any circumstances.

üìä

Selection Criteria

Dissertations and application materials will be evaluated based on their contributions, the potential impact of the research on both theory and practice, and the quality and relevance of publications produced during the Ph.D. studies.

I

Initial Review

All submitted nomination forms (submitted through Google Form) will be reviewed by the selection committee. A merit list will be prepared based on the evaluation.

II

Final Presentation

The top three candidates from the merit list will be invited to present their research to a panel during IEEE SEFET-2026 in Nagpur. Candidates must register for and attend IEEE SEFET-2026 in person.
