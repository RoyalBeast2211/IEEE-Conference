Welcome to IEEE SEFET 2026

IEEE 6th International Conference on Sustainable Energy and Future Electric Transportation (SEFET) aims to provide a platform for Engineers, Researchers, Scientists & Academicians to present their work, to share experiences and ideas in the areas of Renewable Energy, Electric Transportation, Power Electronics, Electric Drives, Control Techniques, Smart Grids, Communication Protocols, Intelligent Charging Infrastructure and Standards, Energy Markets, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.

In view of the changing scenario under sustainable energy & electric transportation, this conference aims to put together the experts from these areas to disseminate their knowledge and experience for working in years to come.

THE IEEE SEFET program will feature Keynotes and Plenary Sessions, Technical Paper Presentations, Industry Sessions, and Awards.

Important Dates

  • Paper submission opens 01st Oct 2025
  • Deadline for full paper submission 31st Jan 2026
  • Notification of acceptance 15th Mar 2026
  • Registration opens 15th Mar 2026
  • Deadline for Early Bird Registration 15th Apr 2026
  • Deadline for Standard Registration 05th Jun 2026
  • Deadline for camera ready paper submission 05th Jun 2026

All the accepted and presented papers will be published in the form of e-proceedings and submitted to IEEE Xplore Digital Library (indexed in SCOPUS, Google Scholar, and other major indexing).

All presented papers will be considered for further review and publication in IEEE Transactions on Industry Applications and IEEE Industry Applications Magazine.

Regular Tracks

  • 1. Renewable Energy Systems, Integration, and Sustainable Technologies
  • 2. Power Electronics, Electrical Machines and Drives, Energy Conversion for Sustainable Transportation
  • 2. Charging Systems, Infrastructure, Planning and Grid Interaction for E-Mobility
  • 4. Energy Storage Technologies, Battery Management, and Hydrogen Storage
  • 5. Power System Planning, Stability, Reliability, and Smart/Microgrid Operations
  • 6. Control Systems, Instrumentation, and Automation in Energy/Power Applications and Industrial Applications
  • 7. Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Analytics, IoT, and Cybersecurity in Power/Energy Systems
  • 8. Energy Markets, Regulatory Aspects, Policies, and Decarbonization Strategies
